.lf-bar

.lf-bar is provided by livefyre-bootstrap. It can hold a .lf-nav, which extends Bootstrap's .nav and when in a .lf-bar matches the LF style guide.

lf-bar has two themes, .lf-bar-default (light) and .lf-bar-inverted (dark)
